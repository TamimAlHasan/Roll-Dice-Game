How To Play

- The game has 2 Players and can be played as Single Player or Multiplayer, playing in rounds.


- In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result gets added to his current score.


- BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his scores will get lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.


- The player can press 'HOLD' which means, his current score gets added to his points panel. After that, it's the next player's turn


- In that case, the points that are saved on the panel will not be lost if the dice rolled to number 1 on the next turn.
